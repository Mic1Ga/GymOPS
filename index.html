FAQ

Common Questions

Straight answers. No waffle.

Do I need any technical skills?
No. If you can open a web browser, you can use GymOS. It's one file that opens in Chrome. The setup wizard walks you through everything in about five minutes.
Where does my data go?
Nowhere. Your data stays on your device in your browser's local storage. There are no servers, no cloud databases, no third parties with access to your member information. It's completely sovereign.
What are the AI running costs?
Typically $20-50 per month for a standard gym, depending on how much content you generate. You use your own API key from Claude (Anthropic), ChatGPT (OpenAI), or other providers. You only pay for what you use.
Can it actually post to my social media?
Yes. GymOS connects directly to Facebook, Instagram (via Meta), Google My Business, WhatsApp, and email through their official APIs. You set up the connections once in Settings, and the Marketing Coordinator handles the rest.
Will the AI content sound like me or like a robot?
During setup, you describe your gym's personality, tone, and values. The AI learns your voice and writes content that sounds like you wrote it. You can review and edit anything before it goes live.
What if I want to stop using it?
If you bought Lite or Pro, the file is yours forever. No subscription to cancel. If you're on Managed, cancel any time — no contracts, no penalties, no lock-in. Export your data and go.
Can I try it before I pay?
Yes. The Pilot Programme includes a 14-day free trial with full access. Click the demo button above to see a working version right now. The demo uses simulated data so you can explore everything.
How is this different from Mindbody or GymCatch?
Those are booking systems. GymOS is a marketing, sales, and retention team. They handle scheduling; GymOS handles getting more people through the door and keeping them there. They work alongside each other, not instead of.

Facebook & Instagram

Connect your Facebook Page and Instagram Business account to post directly from GymOS.

📘

Meta Graph API

Powers both Facebook and Instagram posting
Not Connected
Setup Guide — Facebook & Instagram:
  1. Go to developers.facebook.com and create a Meta Developer account
  2. Click "Create App" → choose "Business" type
  3. Add the "Facebook Login" and "Pages" products to your app
  4. Go to Tools → Graph API Explorer
  5. Select your app, click "Get Token" → "Get Page Access Token"
  6. Grant permissions: pages_manage_posts, pages_read_engagement, instagram_basic, instagram_content_publish
  7. Copy the Page Access Token above
  8. Your Page ID is in your Facebook Page's About section or URL
  9. For Instagram: link your Instagram Business account to your Facebook Page in Meta Business Suite, then use the Graph API Explorer to find your IG Business Account ID
  10. Important: Exchange your short-lived token for a long-lived one (60 days) via: GET /oauth/access_token?grant_type=fb_exchange_token&client_id=APP_ID&client_secret=APP_SECRET&fb_exchange_token=SHORT_TOKEN

Google My Business

Post updates to your Google Business Profile to improve local search visibility.

🔍

Google Business Profile API

Post updates and respond to reviews
Not Connected
Setup Guide — Google My Business:
  1. Go to console.cloud.google.com and create a new project
  2. Enable the "Google My Business API" (now called Business Profile API)
  3. Go to Credentials → Create OAuth 2.0 Client ID (type: Web application)
  4. Add http://localhost as an authorized redirect URI
  5. Copy the Client ID and Client Secret above
  6. Get a refresh token using the OAuth Playground: go to developers.google.com/oauthplayground
  7. Enter scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/business.manage
  8. Click "Authorize APIs" and sign in with the Google account that manages your business
  9. Click "Exchange authorization code for tokens" and copy the Refresh Token
  10. Your Account ID and Location ID can be found via the API or in your Google Business Profile dashboard URL

WhatsApp Business

Send automated messages and respond to enquiries via WhatsApp.

💬

WhatsApp Cloud API (Meta)

Official WhatsApp Business Platform
Not Connected
Setup Guide — WhatsApp Business:
  1. Go to developers.facebook.com (same account as Facebook)
  2. Open your app (or create one) → Add "WhatsApp" product
  3. Go to WhatsApp → Getting Started
  4. You'll see a temporary access token — copy it above
  5. Note the Phone Number ID and WhatsApp Business Account ID shown
  6. For production: create a System User in Business Settings, generate a permanent token with whatsapp_business_messaging permission
  7. Register your business phone number in WhatsApp → Phone Numbers
  8. Create message templates in WhatsApp → Message Templates for automated outreach (templates must be approved by Meta, takes 24-48 hours)
  9. Note: You can reply freely within 24 hours of a customer message. Outside that window, you must use approved templates.

Email

Send newsletters, follow-ups, and automated emails to leads and members.

📧

SendGrid

Reliable email delivery — free tier: 100 emails/day
Not Connected
Setup Guide — SendGrid Email:
  1. Go to sendgrid.com and create a free account
  2. Verify your email address
  3. Go to Settings → API Keys → Create API Key
  4. Choose "Full Access" or "Restricted Access" with Mail Send permission
  5. Copy the API key (starts with SG.) and paste above
  6. Go to Settings → Sender Authentication
  7. Verify your sending domain (add DNS records to your domain) or verify a single sender email
  8. The free tier gives you 100 emails per day — more than enough for most gyms
  9. Alternative: You can also use Mailgun, Amazon SES, or any SMTP service. Contact us for setup help with other providers.
