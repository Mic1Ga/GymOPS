## First-day playbook (Michel, when you sit down to sell)

**Hour 1**: read `MARKET-INTEL-BRIEFING.md` cover to cover. Internalise the 3 key stats: 33.6% churn, $99 MindBody floor, <1% APAC penetration.

**Hour 2-4**: pick 10 gym owners in your network. Send Cold Outreach Sequence A (LinkedIn) or Sequence B (email) tailored to each.

**Hour 5-6**: when first reply lands, run Discovery Call Script. 30 minutes. Booking the demo or qualifying out — both are wins.

**Hour 7-8**: when first demo books, prep using Demo Flow Script. 15 minutes. Close on the trial.

**Hour 9-10**: when first trial signs, send Trial Activation Letter within 5 minutes. Lock the Day 3 check-in.

If you get to a signed Order Form on Day 1 — celebrate. Most don't. Day 7 is realistic. Day 14 is normal.
