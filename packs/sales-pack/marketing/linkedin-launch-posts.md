▼ COPY ▼

Where does your gym member data actually live?

· If you use MindBody: their AWS infrastructure. US-based.
· If you use Glofox: their cloud. Servers across multiple regions.
· If you use HighLevel: their Atlanta data centre.

If you have a Thai member, that data crossed a border to be stored. Under PDPA Section 27 you're obliged to inform them. Section 37(4) gives you 72 hours to report any breach. Section 83 sets the cap at THB 3,000,000.

The Thai PDPC has cumulatively fined organisations THB 21.5M+ by August 2025. The enforcement is real.

Sovereignty isn't a feature. It's an architecture. And the architecture solves the legal problem before the legal problem starts.
