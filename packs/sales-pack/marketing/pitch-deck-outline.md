## Notes for the deck designer

**Palette**: keep it dark + warm. The live tool uses oxblood / brass / cream. Match that — don't introduce new colours.

**Type**: serif for headlines (Libre Baskerville works), sans for body (DM Sans), mono for stats/numbers (IBM Plex Mono).

**Imagery**: avoid stock photos of "fit people smiling at phones." Use:
- Screenshots from the actual tool
- The gym owner's own gym photos (if you have permission)
- Simple diagrams (data flow, before/after)
- Negative space — don't fill every pixel

**Animation**: zero. The deck should print to PDF and still work.

**Footer on every slide except cover**: small text bottom-right — *"GymOps · sovereign by design"*
