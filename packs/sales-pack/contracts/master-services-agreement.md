## 4. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for the period set out in the Order Form ("**Initial Term**"). Subscription plans auto-renew for successive renewal terms equal to the Initial Term unless either Party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 30 days before the end of the then-current term.

4.2 **Termination for convenience.** Either Party may terminate any Subscription Plan with 30 days' written notice. The Owner (perpetual) Plan has no termination for convenience — the licence is for the lifetime of the Software.

4.3 **Termination for cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement with immediate effect if the other Party:
   (a) materially breaches the Agreement and fails to cure within 14 days of written notice;
   (b) becomes insolvent, enters administration, or is wound up;
   (c) violates the Acceptable Use Policy in a manner that exposes Provider to legal or reputational harm.

4.4 **Effect of termination.**
   (a) Provider's obligation to issue new Licence Envelopes ceases.
   (b) Existing Licence Envelopes remain valid for the period they were signed for — i.e. a 12-month envelope continues to work even if the Agreement terminates earlier.
   (c) Client retains permanent access to Client Data (it never left Client's device) and to the underlying open source code.
   (d) Provisions intended to survive (confidentiality, IP, governing law, accrued fees) survive termination.
