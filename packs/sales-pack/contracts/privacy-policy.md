## What this policy covers

This policy explains how we, {{ Gym name }}, collect and use your personal data when you interact with our gym — whether you visit our website, send an enquiry, book a class, or become a member.

We use a gym-operations tool called **GymOps** to help us manage marketing, replies, retention, and admin. GymOps is a special kind of tool: it runs entirely on our own laptops and devices. **Your personal data does not get sent to GymOps as a company.** It stays on our hardware.

---

## What we collect

When you:

- **Send us an enquiry** (web form, WhatsApp, email, DM): your name, contact details, what you asked.
- **Become a member**: your name, contact, emergency contact, payment details (handled by our billing platform, not by us), and any health information you choose to share for safe-training purposes.
- **Attend classes**: attendance records, occasional class feedback.
- **Engage with our marketing** (open emails, click links): we may know that you opened or clicked, depending on the channel.

We do not collect biometric data, location data, or behavioural tracking beyond the basics above.

---

## What we do with it

- **Reply to your enquiry.** AI assists us with drafting fast replies. A human reviews before it goes out.
- **Send you membership communications** (renewal reminders, class changes, safety notices).
- **Send marketing emails / messages** if you've opted in. You can opt out anytime — link in every email or reply STOP.
- **Improve our service.** We may look at aggregated patterns (which classes are full, which marketing channels work) — never your individual data for non-essential purposes.
- **Comply with law** — tax records, accident reports, safety obligations.

---

## Where it's stored

- **Member records, leads, marketing content drafts**: on our own laptops, inside the GymOps tool. **Not in any third-party cloud.**
- **Payments**: handled by {{ billing provider — Stripe / your local provider }}. Their privacy policy governs your payment data.
- **Class bookings**: {{ if separate — MindBody / Glofox / your booking system }}. Their privacy policy governs that data.
- **Backups**: we export weekly to a file we keep on encrypted storage.

---

## Who we share it with

We share your personal data with:

- **AI providers** when our staff use AI to draft replies or content. The provider is one of: Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or Google Gemini. They receive only the specific text needed for the task — not our full member database. They process under their own privacy commitments. (Reply STOP and we'll tag your record to be excluded from AI-assisted communications.)
- **Communication platforms** when we send you a message (your email goes to our email provider; your phone number goes to WhatsApp/Twilio for SMS).
- **Payment processors** — only the data needed to take payment.
- **Authorities** — only when legally required.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it for advertising targeting. We do not allow third-party trackers on our website.

---

## How long we keep it

- **Enquiry data**: 24 months, unless you become a member.
- **Member data**: while you are a member + 36 months after your membership ends (for accounting, dispute resolution, and re-engagement with consent).
- **Marketing list**: until you opt out, then 30 days for confirmation.
- **AI conversation records**: 6 months in the GymOps audit chain (this is for EU AI Act Article 12 compliance — applies if we have any EU/UK members).

You can ask us to delete your data earlier — see below.

---

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have the right to:

- **Access** — ask what we hold about you. We respond within 30 days.
- **Correct** — ask us to fix wrong information.
- **Delete** — ask us to delete your data. We will, unless we're required by law to keep specific records (e.g. tax).
- **Object** — opt out of marketing at any time.
- **Portability** — get a copy of your data in a usable format (we export from GymOps to JSON).
- **Withdraw consent** — anywhere consent is our legal basis.

To exercise any of these: email {{ privacy@gym.com }}.

If you live in:

- **Thailand** — your rights are under PDPA (B.E. 2562 / 2019). Complaints go to the PDPC.
- **Australia** — your rights are under the Privacy Act 1988 and APPs. Complaints go to the OAIC.
- **Indonesia** — your rights are under PDP Law 27/2022.
- **Philippines** — your rights are under DPA 10173. Complaints go to the NPC.
- **Vietnam** — your rights are under PDPL 13/2023.
- **Singapore** — your rights are under PDPA 2012. Complaints go to the PDPC.
- **EU / UK** — your rights are under GDPR / UK GDPR. Complaints go to your local DPA.

## Cross-border transfers

When we use AI to draft a reply or post, the text is sent to the AI provider, which may process it outside your country. This is necessary for the tool to work. We use providers with appropriate cross-border safeguards in place. You can ask us to handle your communications without AI assistance — reply CONTACT to any of our messages.

When we send you SMS or email, the platform handling delivery may be outside your country. This is also unavoidable for the service. Standard industry safeguards apply.

## Security

- Our laptops are password-protected with full-disk encryption.
- The GymOps tool uses your browser's secure storage.
- Backups are encrypted.
- Staff access to member data is on a need-to-know basis.

In the event of a data breach we will notify you and the relevant authority within 72 hours where required by law.

## Children

We do not knowingly collect personal data from people under 16 without parental consent. If you believe we have, please contact us.
