# GymOps · Acceptable Use Policy

**Version 1.0** · effective from the date you activate your trial envelope or paid licence.

This policy tells you what GymOps can and cannot be used for. Breaking it gives us the right to revoke your licence — but since the software runs on your device, "revoke" means we stop signing your envelopes. The software keeps running for whatever time is left on your current envelope.

## You may use GymOps to

- Run the marketing, sales, retention, scheduling, and admin operations of your own gym, studio, or fitness business
- Connect any AI provider (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Google Gemini, etc.) using your own API keys
- Store member data, lead records, content drafts, retention messages, and any other business data in the browser's local storage (IndexedDB) on your device
- Export your data to JSON/CSV for backup, reporting, or transfer to a new device
- Connect third-party services (Meta Business Suite, SendGrid, Twilio, Google Business Profile, WhatsApp Business API, your CRM, your booking system) using your own credentials
- Customise the tone, language, and content generation prompts to match your gym's brand
- Use the audit chain (Article 12) export for any regulatory or compliance audit that asks for record-keeping evidence

## You must not use GymOps to

- **Spam.** Sending unsolicited messages to people who have not opted in to receive them from your gym. This includes WhatsApp blasts to non-members, mass-emailing scraped lists, or auto-DM-ing strangers on social platforms.
- **Resell as a service.** GymOps is licensed to one gym entity per envelope. You may not white-label it and resell it to other gyms unless you have a separate Reseller Agreement signed with us.
- **Process data that doesn't belong to you.** Your members' data, your leads, your content — yes. Someone else's gym's data, scraped lists you bought, data from a former employer — no.
- **Generate content that violates platform rules.** Each platform (Meta, TikTok, Google, etc.) has its own AUP. We don't enforce theirs, but you're responsible for compliance.
- **Generate content that is deceptive, defamatory, discriminatory, harassing, or illegal in your jurisdiction.** The AI will refuse most of this by default. If you find a way around the refusal, we still don't authorise the use.
- **Misrepresent AI-generated content as human-authored where the platform requires disclosure.** EU AI Act Article 50 requires bot disclosure for chatbots. The transparency badge generator inside GymOps exists for this reason — use it.
- **Process special-category data without legal basis.** Health data, biometric data, or sensitive personal data is regulated everywhere. If you collect injury history, body composition scans, or anything similar through GymOps, you must have lawful basis under your jurisdiction's privacy law (PDPA Thailand, Privacy Act Australia, GDPR if any member is in the EU/UK, etc.) and you must disclose this collection to the member.
- **Circumvent the licence envelope.** Patching, decompiling, or otherwise modifying the envelope verification is a breach. We don't pursue most enforcement — but it does void warranty and any support.

## Liability for content

The AI generates content. You publish content. **You are responsible for what gets published.** Review every AI-generated message, post, or auto-reply before it goes out the first time — and spot-check periodically afterwards.

## Reporting abuse

If you believe someone else is using GymOps in violation of this policy — for example, you've received spam that you traced to a GymOps user — email the operator address in your contract. We act on credible reports within 7 days.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be sent to your operator contact email at least 14 days before they take effect. Continuing to use GymOps after the change date constitutes acceptance.

---

**Plain English summary**: use it for your own gym, use it honestly, don't spam, don't lie about it being AI-generated, don't resell it without a deal. Everything else, common sense.
