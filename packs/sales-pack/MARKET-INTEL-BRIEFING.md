## 5. Legal — Verified Specifics

### Thailand PDPA (B.E. 2562 / 2019) — ENFORCEMENT IS REAL
- **Section 83 fine**: up to THB 3,000,000 (~USD 81,000) for breach-notification failure
- **Section 37(4)**: 72-hour breach reporting clock
- **Cumulative enforcement to August 2025**: THB ~21.5M across public + private sector cases
- **Cross-border transfers** (Dec 2023 / March 2024 regs): require SCCs based on ASEAN or EU models updated with Thai-specific obligations including 72-hour clock
- **What this means for GymOps**: when Client uses an LLM (Claude/OpenAI/Gemini) the call IS a cross-border transfer. Client needs lawful basis + contract safeguards with the LLM provider. Our DPA addresses this.
- **Source**: [FOSR Law analysis](https://fosrlaw.com/2025/thailand-pdpa-compliance-enforcement-cross-border-transfers/) · DLA Piper · Hogan Lovells · Tilleke & Gibbins (7+ independent confirmations)

### Vietnam PDPL 2025 (Law 91/2025/QH15) — NEW LAW
- **Effective date**: January 1, 2026
- **Extraterritorial scope**: applies to any foreign SaaS processing personal data of Vietnamese citizens
- **Penalty cap**: up to 5% of preceding-year revenue for unlawful cross-border transfers
- **Penalty floor for other violations**: VND 3 billion (~USD 114,500-118,000)
- **What this means for GymOps**: sell into Vietnam from Jan 1 2026 = you are in scope. DPA needs Vietnam clause.
- **Source**: [Vietnam Business Law](https://vietnam-business-law.info/blog/2025/10/1/key-highlights-on-the-vietnam-new-personal-data-protection-law-pdpl-2025) + Tilleke & Gibbins + Rouse + IAPP + Lexology

### ⚠️ TWO CLAIMS WERE REFUTED IN ADVERSARIAL VERIFICATION — DO NOT REPEAT
1. ❌ **"Every Vietnamese organisation must appoint a DPO under PDPL 2025"** — refuted 0-3. The DPO requirement is not universal; selective by data type/processing scope.
2. ❌ **"On-device storage avoids Transfer Impact Assessment (TIA) obligations under PDPL 2025"** — refuted 0-3. TIA obligations attach to the cross-border transfer event (e.g. LLM API call), not to where the data was previously stored.

**Use neither of these claims in sales conversations or marketing.**
