# GymOps · 15-Minute Demo Flow

**Audience**: gym owner, GM, or marketing manager. Single decision-maker preferred.
**Goal**: by minute 15 they say either *"yes, send me the trial"* or *"not for me right now"*. Both are wins — no ghost-followups.

---

## Pre-call (5 min, not on the call)

- [ ] Pull up the live demo URL on your screen: `https://sjgant80-hub.github.io/gymos/clients/gymops.html`
- [ ] Have it loaded · cleared of test data
- [ ] Know their gym name, programmes (CrossFit / yoga / boxing / mixed), and member count
- [ ] Open the Settings panel with your AI Engine key already pasted (saves 30 seconds)

---

## Minute 0 — 1 · Frame

> **"Thanks for the 15 minutes. Here's how I'd like to spend them. First 5 minutes I show you what GymOps does. Then 5 minutes I show you the parts most gym owners care about. Then 5 minutes for your questions and a yes / no on whether this is something you want to try free for 30 days. Sound fair?"**

(Wait for them to say yes — they always do.)

---

## Minute 1 — 5 · The 5-minute show

Share screen · open the live URL.

> *"This is one URL. There's no app, no install, no monthly account in some cloud somewhere. The whole gym ops system runs in your browser tab. Your data stays on your device. I'll prove it later."*

Click through **landing page only**:
- Hero — "Your Gym Gets a Full AI Team"
- Scroll to **Team grid** — show the 8 specialists + Director
- Click **Director** card — show what it does (routes the team)
- Scroll to **FAQ** — point at "Where does my data live?" answer
- Scroll to **footer** — copyright only (no jargon · they shouldn't ask about this)

> *"Eight roles, one director. Each does one of the things gym owners hate doing manually. Marketing, retention, lead capture, content, scheduling, all of it."*

---

## Minute 5 — 10 · The parts they care about

Click **"See It Working"** → app loads.

**Then walk these 3 specific flows** (in this order — it builds the case):

### Flow 1 · Lead capture (Front Desk specialist)
- Click **Leads tab**
- Add a demo lead with their gym's actual programme as context
- Show how the AI categorises it (cold/warm/hot)
- Show the auto-reply suggestion

> *"Front Desk catches every enquiry · 24/7. Most gyms lose 30% of enquiries because nobody replies in time. This replies in 30 seconds, even at 2am."*

### Flow 2 · Content engine (Content Manager specialist)
- Click **Content tab**
- Generate a post for their actual programme: type something like *"Member success story for our CrossFit class"*
- Show 3 platform-specific variants (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn) generated
- Click **📋 Copy** to show the copy-to-clipboard

> *"This is the part that costs you $300-500/month if you outsource to an agency. Or 4 hours of your week if you do it yourself. GymOps does it in 30 seconds and it sounds like you because it learned your tone of voice from your last 5 posts."*

### Flow 3 · Retention (Retention Specialist)
- Click **Dashboard** → show the "at-risk members" panel
- Pretend they have a member who hasn't checked in for 14 days
- Show the auto-generated retention message

> *"The number-one reason members churn is silence. Three weeks go by, they get embarrassed, they don't come back. This catches them at week 2."*

---

## Minute 10 — 13 · The "where's the catch" part

This is the most important 3 minutes.

> *"Now — three questions you're probably thinking. Let me answer them before you have to ask."*

**Q1 · Where does my data actually live?**

Open DevTools → Application → IndexedDB → show their gym's database.

> *"There. That's it. No server in San Francisco, no Australian cloud, no Thai data centre. Your members never leave your laptop. If you close the browser, they're still there. If you switch to a new laptop, you export and import a single .json file."*

**Q2 · What happens if I stop paying you?**

> *"Worst case scenario for you is: the AI stops generating posts. Everything else — your members, leads, content history, retention messages — keeps working forever. The URL doesn't expire. You own the data. We just stop signing the AI envelope."*

**Q3 · What if you go out of business?**

> *"Same answer. The tool still runs. The AI cascade falls back to whatever provider you've connected (Claude, OpenAI, whichever you bought a key for). The code is on GitHub — open source — so anyone can keep maintaining it. There's no central kill-switch."*

## Minute 13 — 15 · The ask

> *"Here's where I am. The trial is 30 days free, no card. I set you up tonight, I walk you through it Wednesday, we do a 14-day check-in. If by day 14 you can tell me this thing has saved you time — and most owners save 8-12 hours a week — you pay [$X/month or $XXX one-time]. If not, you walk and we delete the licence.*

> *Do you want me to send you the trial envelope tonight?"*

---

## Three responses you might get · and how to handle

**"Yes, send it" →** Send the activation letter (see `onboarding/trial-activation-letter.md`) within 5 minutes. Lock in the Wednesday call before you hang up.

**"I need to think about it" →** *"Totally fair. Two questions — what would make this a yes? And when should I check back?"* If they can't answer the first one, the real answer is no. Don't follow up more than once.

**"How much is it?" before you've gotten through Flow 3 →** *"I'll send you a clean pricing sheet — but honestly, the price isn't the thing. The thing is whether the system actually does what I just showed you for YOUR gym. Let's finish the demo, then I'll tell you whatever you want about price."*
