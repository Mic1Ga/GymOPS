## The pricing logic

GymOps is positioned at **half the floor price of the verified competitive anchor (MindBody Starter $99/month per location)**. Real-world MindBody quotes are commonly **$139-$159/month** once SMS, transactional charges, and branded-app fees are bundled. We deliberately stay below that — we don't need MindBody's per-member CRM scale or POS sophistication, so we don't carry their cost structure.

**Source for MindBody anchor**: [mindbodyonline.com/business/pricing](https://www.mindbodyonline.com/business/pricing) (Starter $99 published) · multiple secondary sources confirm real-world $139-$159 effective quotes.

The **Owner plan** breaks even against Standard at **~18 months** — designed for owners who hate subscriptions and want capital expenditure over operational expenditure. For Asia/Pacific gym owners who pay USD for every Western SaaS subscription, the perpetual option is meaningful — it ends the **"SE Asia gym tax."**
