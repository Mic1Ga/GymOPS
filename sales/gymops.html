questions

The honest FAQ.

Where does my member data live?

In your browser's IndexedDB on the device you use. There's no cloud database we own. No backup is automatic — we'll show you how to export to a JSON file on Day 3 so you have your own backup. If your laptop dies, you restore from that file on the new one.

What happens if I stop paying you?

The AI features stop generating new content. Everything else — your members, leads, content history, retention messages — keeps working. The URL doesn't expire. You own the data. We just stop signing the AI envelope.

What if GymOps goes out of business?

The source code is open on GitHub under MIT licence. The tool keeps running. The AI cascade falls back to whatever provider you've connected directly. The code is forkable. There's no central kill-switch.

Do you replace MindBody or run alongside?

Most owners keep MindBody (or Glofox) for class booking and billing — those are sticky. We replace the marketing, content, retention, and CRM layer that most enterprise tools do badly. You'd run both for a while.

How is this $49 when MindBody charges $99-159?

We don't have your stack's cost structure. There's no backend cloud bill (your data is on your laptop). No per-member fees (we don't count members). No enterprise account team. It's lean by design.

Is the AI any good?

It uses whichever model you BYOK to — Claude, OpenAI, Gemini. You pay the model provider directly for inference (typically pennies per post). The AI learns your gym's tone of voice from your past posts in about 5 minutes. The first content is good. The 100th is indistinguishable from yours.

What about GDPR / PDPA / Australian Privacy Act?

Sovereign architecture makes most of this easier, not harder. Your members never leave your device. Cross-border concerns drop out. We ship a Data Processing Addendum template before you sign · adapt it to your jurisdiction with your privacy lawyer.
